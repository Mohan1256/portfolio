About Me

I am a passionate 3D modeler with a strong interest in game development, always striving to create immersive and realistic experiences. My journey into 3D modeling began with a fascination for intricate designs and textures, which led me to explore Blender and develop my skills in sculpting, rigging, lighting, rendering, and shading. I am also eager to expand my knowledge in Unity, where I’m learning how to bring my 3D models to life in interactive game environments. Although I’m still early in my career, my enthusiasm for continuous learning drives me to take on various projects that help me improve my techniques and broaden my expertise. I’m dedicated to mastering both the creative and technical aspects of 3D modeling and game development.
